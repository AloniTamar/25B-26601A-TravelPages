1. The Eiffel Tower

This iconic iron lattice tower offers breathtaking views of the city from its top deck, and is especially romantic at sunset or twilight. You can even dine at the Michelin-starred Le Jules Verne on the second floor!



3. Montmartre

This charming, bohemian neighborhood is a maze of narrow streets, charming cafes, and historic artist studios. Take a stroll to the top of the hill, where you'll find the beautiful Sacré-Cœur Basilica and enjoy panoramic views of the city.
