1. The Eiffel Tower

The iconic iron lattice tower is an engineering marvel and offers breathtaking views of the city from its observation decks. Take the elevator or stairs to the top for an unforgettable experience.



2. The Louvre Museum

One of the world's largest and most famous museums, the Louvre is home to an impressive collection of art and artifacts, including the Mona Lisa, Venus de Milo, and Egyptian antiquities. Be prepared to spend hours exploring the vast galleries and exhibitions.



3. Montmartre

Perched on a hill in the heart of Paris, Montmartre is a charming, bohemian neighborhood with narrow streets, charming cafes, and stunning views of the city. Visit the Basilique du Sacré-Cœur, explore the artist studios, and soak up the romantic atmosphere.
