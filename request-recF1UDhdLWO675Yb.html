1. The Eiffel Tower

This iconic iron lattice tower is a symbol of Paris and one of the most recognizable landmarks in the world, offering breathtaking views of the city from its observation decks. You can take the stairs or elevator to the top for an unforgettable experience.



2. The Louvre Museum

One of the world's largest and most famous museums, the Louvre is home to an impressive collection of art and artifacts from ancient civilizations to the 19th century, including the Mona Lisa. The stunning glass pyramid entrance and beautifully restored royal palace add to the museum's allure.



3. Montmartre

This charming historic neighborhood is famous for its bohemian vibe, street artists, and stunning views of the city from the top of the hill. Wander the narrow alleys, visit the beautiful Sacré-Cœur Basilica, and enjoy a coffee or lunch at a classic Parisian café.
