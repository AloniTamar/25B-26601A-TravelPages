1. The Eiffel Tower

The iconic Eiffel Tower is an iron lattice tower that offers breathtaking views of the city from its top deck. Visitors can take the elevator or stairs to reach the top for an unforgettable experience.



2. The Louvre Museum

The world-renowned Louvre Museum is home to an impressive collection of art and artifacts from around the world, including the famous Mona Lisa painting. The museum's stunning glass pyramid entrance is a work of art in itself.



3. Montmartre

Montmartre is a charming historic neighborhood known for its bohemian vibe, charming cafes, and stunning views of the city from the top of the hill. Visitors can explore the narrow streets, visit the beautiful Sacré-Cœur Basilica, and enjoy the lively artist community.
