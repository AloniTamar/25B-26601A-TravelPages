Top travel recommendations for London

Kew Gardens

These gardens are a peaceful oasis in the heart of the city and offer a romantic stroll through beautifully maintained grounds, including a treetop walk and a tranquil lake. Visitors can explore the vast collections of plants from around the world, taking in the sights and sounds of the gardens. The gardens also feature several romantic spots, including the Palm House and the Japanese Gateway. As the sun sets, the gardens take on a magical quality, making it an ideal spot for a romantic evening out.

The View from The Shard

For a romantic evening with a view, head to The View from The Shard, which offers breathtaking panoramic views of the city from the 72nd floor of the Shard building. As the sun sets, the views become even more magical, with the twinkling lights of the city below. Visitors can enjoy a drink or a meal at one of the on-site restaurants or bars, taking in the stunning views while savoring the moment.

Hampstead Heath

For a more laid-back romantic evening, head to Hampstead Heath, which offers stunning views of the city and a peaceful atmosphere. Visitors can take a romantic stroll through the Heath, enjoying the beautifully maintained gardens and taking in the sights and sounds of the surrounding area. As the sun sets, the Heath takes on a romantic quality, making it an ideal spot for a relaxing evening out.
