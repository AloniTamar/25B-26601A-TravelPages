Top travel recommendations for London

St Paul's Cathedral

This beautiful cathedral was designed by Christopher Wren and features a stunning dome that offers breathtaking views of the city. You and your partner can take a romantic stroll up to the top and marvel at the intricate architecture. Afterwards, take a walk around the picturesque courtyard and enjoy a picnic lunch. This historic landmark is a must-visit for any couple

The Delaunay

This elegant restaurant is a must-visit for any foodie couple. With its beautifully decorated interior and impeccable service, you'll feel like royalty. The menu features a range of European dishes, from classic Wiener Schnitzel to decadent pastries. Be sure to book in advance, as this popular spot fills up quickly. The perfect place to celebrate a special occasion

View on Map

Hyde Park

Take a romantic stroll through Hyde Park, one of London's largest and most beautiful green spaces. You can walk around the Serpentine Lake, rent a boat and enjoy a picnic on the grass, or visit the Diana Memorial Fountain. This peaceful oasis in the heart of the city is perfect for a relaxing afternoon with your partner. Don't miss the stunning views of Kensington Palace from the park's southern edge
