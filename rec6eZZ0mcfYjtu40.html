Top travel recommendations for Paris

Montmartre

Stroll through the winding streets and discover the artistic charm of Montmartre. This historic neighborhood is famous for its stunning views of the city, charming cafes, and vibrant artists. Visit the Basilique du Sacré-Cœur, explore the Place du Tertre and get lost in the romantic atmosphere. Don’t forget to stop by the Musée de Montmartre.

Palais-Royal

Discover the tranquil atmosphere of the Palais-Royal, a former royal palace with beautiful gardens. Stroll through the courtyard and admire the modern fountains and sculptures. Don’t miss the stunning views of the Louvre Museum and the Seine River. The Palais-Royal is also home to the contemporary art space, the Galerie nationale du Palais-Royal. and the Musée des Égouts de Paris.

Île de la Cité

Explore the heart of Paris, the Île de la Cité, an island in the Seine River. Admire the stunning views of the river, visit the famous Notre-Dame Cathedral and the Sainte-Chapelle. Take a romantic stroll along the Seine and discover the beautiful bridges and the picturesque streets.
