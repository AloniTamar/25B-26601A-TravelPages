Top travel recommendations for Amsterdam

Vondelpark

Stroll hand-in-hand through the beautiful Vondelpark, the largest park in Amsterdam. Admire the picturesque gardens, lakes, and walking paths. Relax on the grass and enjoy a romantic picnic. It's a peaceful oasis in the heart of the city.

Bloemendaal Beach

Escape the city and head to the picturesque Bloemendaal beach, where you can enjoy a romantic walk on the beach, watch the sunset, and relax in the dunes. It's a beautiful natural setting for a romantic day out. You can also take a bike ride through the scenic dunes. It's a must-visit spot for nature lovers.

View on Map

Hortus Botanicus

Step into the tranquil Hortus Botanicus, one of the oldest botanical gardens in the world. Admire the stunning glasshouses, beautiful gardens, and romantic fountains. Take a leisurely stroll through the picturesque gardens and enjoy the peaceful atmosphere.
