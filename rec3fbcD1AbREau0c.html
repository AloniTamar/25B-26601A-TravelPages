Top travel recommendations for Paris

Septime

This cozy restaurant is a foodie's paradise, serving creative, farm-to-table dishes that change seasonally. The intimate setting and exceptional service make it a truly special experience. Be sure to book well in advance, as it's a popular spot among locals and visitors alike. Septime's focus on natural, organic ingredients results in dishes that are both beautiful and delicious.

Breizh Café

This beloved café is a must-visit for anyone craving a delicious, affordable meal in the heart of the city. Their crepes are made with high-quality, fresh ingredients, and their menu offers a range of savory and sweet options. The cozy, rustic atmosphere and friendly service make it an ideal spot to people-watch and relax. Be sure to try one of their signature Bretagne-style crepes, like the classic ham, egg, and cheese.

Le Grand Vefour

This stunning, 18th-century eatery is located in the heart of Paris, offering an unforgettable dining experience. The opulent decor, complete with crystal chandeliers and ornate mirrors, provides a regal atmosphere. The menu features exquisite, haute-cuisine dishes made with the finest, freshest ingredients, and the impeccable service makes it an ideal spot for special occasions. Be sure to reserve a table in advance, as it's a favorite among locals and visitors alike.
