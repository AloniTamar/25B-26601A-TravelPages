Discover the City of Love: Paris

  • The Eiffel Tower

    Experience breathtaking views of the city from the top of the Eiffel Tower.

  • The Louvre Museum

    Explore an impressive collection of art and artefacts, including the Mona Lisa, at the world-renowned Louvre Museum.

  • Montmartre

    Wander through the charming streets of Montmartre, visit the Sacré-Cœur Basilica, and enjoy a romantic dinner at a sidewalk café.
