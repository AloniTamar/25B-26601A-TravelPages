Top travel recommendations for London

Kew Gardens

These beautiful gardens are a peaceful oasis in the city. Take a romantic stroll through the greenhouses and admire the stunning flowers and trees. The gardens also feature several tranquil lakes and walking trails. A perfect spot for a romantic picnic.

View on Map

The Shard

Enjoy a romantic dinner at one of the Shard's many excellent restaurants, with breathtaking views of the city from the top floor. The Shard is a unique and modern symbol of London, and the views are simply stunning. You can even book a private dining room for an extra special evening.

View on Map

Little Venice

This charming area is where the Grand Union and Regent's Canals meet, and is a perfect spot for a romantic stroll or boat ride. Take in the beautiful canal-side scenery and enjoy the many cafes and restaurants in the area.
