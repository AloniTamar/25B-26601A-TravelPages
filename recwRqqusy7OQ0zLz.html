Top travel recommendations for London

Kew Gardens

Escape the city chaos and stroll hand-in-hand through the tranquil landscapes of Kew Gardens. As you wander, take in the breathtaking views of the Palm House, a glasshouse bursting with tropical plants. Visit the Treetop Walkway for panoramic views of the gardens. End your day with a picnic on the Great Broad Walk.

Hampstead Heath

Take a romantic stroll across Hampstead Heath, offering breathtaking views of the city. This former ancient forest boasts scenic walking trails, picnic areas, and stunning vistas from the top of Parliament Hill.

View on Map

Little Venice

Admire the picturesque canals, colorful houseboats, and elegant Regency architecture of Little Venice. Take a romantic stroll or boat ride along the Regent's Canal, and enjoy a quiet dinner at one of the area's quaint cafes.
