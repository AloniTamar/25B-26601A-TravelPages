Top travel recommendations for London

Tower of London

The Tower of London is a symbol of power, love, and sacrifice. Stroll around the majestic architecture and get lost in the romantic atmosphere. You can also witness the Ceremony of the Keys, which has taken place every night for over 700 years. Visit the Crown Jewels, and explore the history of the monarchs and their consorts.

Sky Garden

The Sky Garden is a free public garden on the 35th floor of the Walkie-Talkie building, offering panoramic views of the city from a tropical garden setting. Enjoy a romantic breakfast or lunch, and don't forget to book your tickets in advance as it can get quite crowded. Take a romantic stroll around the gardens, and don't miss the glass walkways with views straight down to the ground.
